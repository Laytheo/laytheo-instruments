## Description

History is the umbrella discipline that investigates the human past, evaluating sources (textual, material, oral) to construct accounts of what happened, why, and what it meant. As a modern academic discipline it took shape in the nineteenth century with Ranke's emphasis on archival research and critical method, while drawing on a much older tradition of historical writing reaching back to Herodotus and Thucydides. The discipline encompasses many specializations by period (ancient, medieval, modern), region, and theme (political, economic, social, cultural, intellectual).
