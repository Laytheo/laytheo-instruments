## Description

Old Testament Studies investigates the books of the Hebrew Bible (the Tanakh) using historical, literary, philological, and theological methods. It attends to questions of composition, redaction, and canon; the historical and cultural context of ancient Israel; and the theological themes that run across the corpus. The field has been shaped by the documentary hypothesis, form criticism, the canonical approach, and increasing engagement with Ancient Near Eastern parallels.
