## Description

Biblical Theology attends to the theological message of Scripture as it develops across the canon, attending to the categories, vocabulary, and structures of the biblical text itself rather than imposing later systematic categories. It commonly works through canonical, redemptive-historical, or thematic approaches, tracing how earlier revelation finds its fulfillment in later. The discipline sits between exegesis and systematic theology, depending on the first and informing the second.
