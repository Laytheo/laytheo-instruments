## Description

Theology Proper treats the doctrine of God as such, distinguished within systematic theology from Christology and Pneumatology by its focus on the divine being, attributes (simplicity, aseity, eternity, immutability, omnipotence, omniscience, goodness), and the eternal relations of the three divine persons. It draws heavily on natural theology and metaphysics in its treatment of the divine attributes and on biblical and patristic sources in its treatment of the Trinity.
