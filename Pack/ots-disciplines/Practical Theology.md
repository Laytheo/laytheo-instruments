## Description

Practical Theology brings theological reflection to bear on the lived practices of the Church and the Christian: preaching, pastoral care, discipleship, worship, mission, and ethics in concrete situations. It treats practice as a genuine source of theological insight rather than merely as the application of doctrine arrived at elsewhere. The discipline includes subfields such as homiletics, poimenics (pastoral care), and missiology.
