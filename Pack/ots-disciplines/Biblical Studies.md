## Description

Biblical Studies is the umbrella discipline encompassing the academic study of the Hebrew Bible, New Testament, and related literature. It draws on philology, history, archaeology, literary criticism, and theology to investigate the formation, transmission, and meaning of biblical texts. The discipline includes confessional and non-confessional approaches and serves as the parent for more specialized fields such as Old Testament Studies, New Testament Studies, and Textual Criticism.
