## Description

Soteriology treats the nature, accomplishment, and application of salvation: how the work of Christ addresses sin and its consequences, and how the benefits of that work are communicated to persons. Its central topics include atonement (with its various models: ransom, satisfaction, penal substitution, Christus Victor, moral influence), justification, sanctification, adoption, and glorification. Soteriology stands at the center of the Reformation debates and continues to be a primary site of theological development across traditions.
