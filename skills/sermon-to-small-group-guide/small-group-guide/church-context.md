# Church context

Fill in the values below. The `small-group-guide` skill reads this
file when generating a guide and uses these values to calibrate language,
identify the speaker in the transcript, and match your congregation's voice.

If you leave fields blank or unfilled, the skill will fall back to a generic
Protestant default and warn you. The guide will still be usable, just less
faithful to your specific context.

## Primary teaching pastor

Pastor's full name. Note other elders or regular guest preachers if helpful.

## Congregational vocabulary

Phrases your church uses regularly. Examples (delete and replace):

- "the gospel"
- "the Lord"
- "brothers and sisters"
- "covenant community"
- "the Word"

## Theological frame

Distinctive emphases of your church's preaching and teaching ministry.
Examples (delete and replace):

- Scripture as authoritative
- Christ-centered preaching
- Emphasis on grace, sanctification, and life-on-life discipleship

## Audience for the guide

Who is reading this on Tuesday night? Default is small group / community
group leaders, mostly lay leaders, facilitating discussion in homes during
the week. Adjust if your context is different.
