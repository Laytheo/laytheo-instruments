## Description

Theology is the umbrella discipline encompassing all rigorous reflection on God, divine revelation, and the relation of created reality to its Creator. As a field it spans confessional, historical, philosophical, and practical modes of inquiry, and serves as the parent category for more specialized theological disciplines such as Christology, Ecclesiology, and Eschatology. Distinct from religious studies (which treats religion as a phenomenon to be observed), theology proceeds from within a tradition of faith seeking understanding.
