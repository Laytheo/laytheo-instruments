## Description

Philosophy is the umbrella discipline encompassing rigorous reflection on the most general and basic questions humans can ask: what exists, what we can know, how we should live, what is beautiful, what makes an argument valid. It is distinguished from the empirical sciences by its concern with foundational and conceptual questions rather than experimental ones, and from theology by its working primarily from natural reason rather than revelation. Philosophy serves as the parent discipline for branches such as Metaphysics, Epistemology, Ethics, and Logic.
